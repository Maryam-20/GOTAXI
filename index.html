WHAT IS GOTAXI

GoTaxi is an application on mobile devices (phones & tablets) that helps Amsterdam residents get around the city and surrounding areas. GoTaxi is available for all Android users and comes packed with features: Go-Moto (Motor), Go-Car (Taxi Online), GoFood, Go-Mart, GoMassage, GoBox, GoSend, and GoService. GoTaxi is here to make Amsterdam life easier.
